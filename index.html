About

I am a Graduate Student in Information Systems and Computer Security at Tuskegee University. My research interests lie at the intersection of Cyber-Physical Systems, Reinforcement Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity.

Currently, I work on the security and resilience of cloud-based cyber-physical systems, with a particular focus on learning-based control systems operating under adversarial and noisy environments.

Before beginning graduate studies in the United States, I served in the Bangladesh Civil Service, holding administrative and policy-making positions under the Ministry of Public Administration, the Ministry of Land, and the Ministry of Commerce. My professional experience in public administration and digital governance motivates my interest in secure, trustworthy, and resilient intelligent systems.

I received my B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).
