About

I am a PhD student working at the intersection of cyber-physical systems, reinforcement learning, and cybersecurity. My research explores how learning-based agents can make critical infrastructure more secure, resilient, and trustworthy — including work that combines AI and blockchain for cyber-physical systems.

Previously, I completed my undergraduate studies in engineering. I am broadly interested in building systems that are both intelligent and verifiable.

Research Interests

  • Cyber-Physical Systems — security, resilience, and control of networked physical infrastructure.
  • Reinforcement Learning — sequential decision-making for autonomous and adaptive systems.
  • Cybersecurity — defending critical systems against adversarial threats.
  • AI + Blockchain — trustworthy, decentralized coordination for CPS.
